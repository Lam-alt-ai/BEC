Chinese Water Dragon

Habitat: Tropical rainforests near streams and rivers

Diet: Insects, worms, small fish, and leafy vegetables

Traits: Long muscular tail for swimming, powerful legs for climbing

Conservation Status: Least Concern, but habitat destruction is a growing threat.
